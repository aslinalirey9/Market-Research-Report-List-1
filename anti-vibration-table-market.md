<p><strong>The Anti-vibration Table Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Anti-vibration Table</li><li>Active Anti-vibration Table</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-vibration table market comprises two main types: passive and active anti-vibration tables. Passive anti-vibration tables use materials like rubber or sorbothane to absorb vibrations without requiring external energy, making them cost-effective and low-maintenance. In contrast, active anti-vibration tables employ advanced technology, including sensors and actuators, to detect vibrations in real-time and counteract them dynamically. While active tables provide precision and adaptability, passive tables are simpler and suitable for environments with moderate vibration levels. Each type serves specific applications in laboratories and manufacturing settings.</p></p>
