<p><p>The Water Algae Online Monitor market encompasses various types of sensors that measure specific algae-related parameters. Chlorophyll-a sensors detect the concentration of this pivotal pigment in algae, indicating biomass levels. FDOM (Fluorescent Dissolved Organic Matter) sensors measure organic matter linked to algal growth. Rhodamine sensors identify specific dye concentrations used for tracking water quality. Phycocyanin and phycoerythrin sensors analyze pigments found in cyanobacteria and red algae, respectively, providing insights into harmful algal blooms and overall aquatic health.</p></p>
