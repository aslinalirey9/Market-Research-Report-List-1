<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electromechanical Solenoid Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Acting Solenoid Valves</li><li>Sub-Step Direct-Acting Solenoid Valves</li><li>Pilot Solenoid Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromechanical solenoid valves market comprises several types, including direct-acting solenoid valves, sub-step direct-acting solenoid valves, and pilot solenoid valves. Direct-acting solenoid valves operate directly with the solenoid to control fluid flow, suitable for low-pressure applications. Sub-step direct-acting solenoid valves offer more precise control through incremental movement. Pilot solenoid valves utilize a smaller solenoid to control a larger volume of fluid, often used in high-pressure systems, allowing for efficient and responsive operation in various industrial applications.</p></p>
