<p><ul><li>Classic Spar</li><li>Truss Spar</li><li>Cell Spar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Point Anchor Reservoir (SPAR) markets are classified into three main types: Classic Spar, Truss Spar, and Cell Spar. Classic Spar features a cylindrical hull with a single mooring point, offering stability in deep waters. Truss Spar employs a truss structure for enhanced structural integrity and resistance to harsh environmental conditions. Cell Spar, similar to Classic Spar, has multiple buoyancy cells and is designed for better load distribution and fatigue resistance. Each type serves specific offshore needs based on water depth and environmental challenges.</p></p>
