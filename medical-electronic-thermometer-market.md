<p><p>The medical electronic thermometer market comprises several types, each designed for specific uses. Oral thermometers measure temperature in the mouth, providing accurate readings for adults and older children. Underarm thermometers are used for quick readings under the arm, ideal for infants. Forehead thermometers offer non-invasive, quick measurements through infrared technology, suitable for all ages. Ear thermometers utilize infrared sensors to assess temperature via the ear canal, while anal thermometers provide the most accurate readings for infants, though less commonly used in older children.</p></p>
