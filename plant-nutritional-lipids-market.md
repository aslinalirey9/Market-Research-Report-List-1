<p><p>The plant nutritional lipids market comprises two main types: phospholipids and sterols. Phospholipids are essential components of cell membranes, contributing to cellular health and stability, and are widely used in food, cosmetics, and dietary supplements. Sterols, particularly phytosterols, are plant-derived compounds that help lower cholesterol levels and support heart health, gaining traction in functional foods and nutraceuticals. The rising demand for plant-based products and health-conscious consumers is driving growth in these lipid segments.</p></p>
