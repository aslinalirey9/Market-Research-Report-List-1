<p><p>The fire protection coating market is primarily divided into two types: liquid fire-resistant coatings and powder fire-resistant coatings. Liquid fire-resistant coatings are versatile and can be easily applied to various surfaces, providing robust fire protection through a reactive process upon exposure to heat. In contrast, powder fire-resistant coatings offer a durable finish that is typically applied using a spray technique, curing to form a strong, protective layer. Both types enhance fire safety across numerous industries and applications.</p></p>
