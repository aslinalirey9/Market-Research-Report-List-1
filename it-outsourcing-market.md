In contrast, application outsourcing focuses on the development, management, and maintenance of software applications by external vendors, enabling businesses to leverage specialized expertise and enhance operational efficiency. Both models allow organizations to concentrate on core functions while benefiting from outsourced capabilities and innovations.</p></p>
