<p><p>The Distributed Feedback (DFB) laser chip market includes two main types: free space laser diodes and fiber-coupled laser diodes. Free space laser diodes emit light directly into the air, making them suitable for applications like free-space optics and communication systems where line-of-sight is feasible. In contrast, fiber-coupled laser diodes are designed to connect directly to optical fibers, providing efficient light transmission for telecommunications, sensing, and data transmission applications. Both types are essential for various high-speed optical technologies.</p></p>
