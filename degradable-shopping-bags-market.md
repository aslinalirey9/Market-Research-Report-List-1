<p><p>Degradable shopping bags are categorized into three primary market types: organic waste, home compost, and industrial compost. Organic waste bags are designed to break down in natural environments and are suitable for municipal waste systems. Home compost bags are optimized for backyard composting, decomposing easily in household compost setups. Industrial compost bags require commercial facilities for effective breakdown, as they often need specific temperatures and conditions to decompose. Each type addresses sustainability while catering to different disposal methods and settings.</p></p>
