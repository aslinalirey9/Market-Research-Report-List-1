<p><strong>The Gaseous Ionization Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Gas-flow Proportional Detector"</li><li>"Sealed Tubes Proportional Detector"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gaseous ionization detector market encompasses two primary types: gas-flow proportional detectors and sealed tubes proportional detectors. Gas-flow proportional detectors operate by allowing a continuous flow of gas through the detection chamber, where ionization occurs upon radiation interaction, leading to measurable electrical signals. In contrast, sealed tubes proportional detectors contain a fixed volume of gas sealed within a tube, providing a compact and efficient design for detecting ionization. Both types are utilized in various applications, including radiation monitoring and analytical measurement.</p></p>
