<p><p>The multi-material packaging market includes several key materials, each with unique properties. Polyethylene (PE) offers flexibility and moisture resistance, while Polypropylene (PP) is known for its durability and heat resistance. Polyamide (PA) provides excellent barrier properties, making it suitable for food packaging. Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH) is valued for its gas barrier characteristics, often used in food preservation. Lastly, Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is widely used for its strength and clarity, commonly found in beverage containers and packaging applications.</p></p>
