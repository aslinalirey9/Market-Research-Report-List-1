<p><ul><li>Single-mode Switches</li><li>Multimode Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-optical switches market encompasses two primary types: single-mode switches and multimode switches. Single-mode switches are designed for high-speed, long-distance transmission, utilizing a single light pathway to minimize signal loss and interference. In contrast, multimode switches cater to shorter distances, using multiple pathways to support simultaneous signal transmission. Both types serve critical roles in telecommunications, data centers, and networking applications, enabling efficient data transfer and enhancing overall network performance through optical switching technologies.</p></p>
