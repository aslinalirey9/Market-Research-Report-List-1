<p><p>The bolt tension monitor market comprises various testing types, primarily ultrasonic testing and sensor testing. Ultrasonic testing uses high-frequency sound waves to assess bolt integrity, detecting flaws or inconsistencies in tension levels effectively. In contrast, sensor testing employs electronic devices to measure and monitor the tension in bolts, offering real-time data on their performance. Both methods are essential for ensuring the safety and reliability of structures, as they help prevent failure due to improper bolt tensioning.</p></p>
