<p><p>The overlapping sliding door system market encompasses two primary types: single opening and double opening configurations. Single opening doors operate on a single track, allowing one panel to slide open while the other remains stationary, maximizing space efficiency for smaller entryways. In contrast, double opening doors consist of two panels that slide in opposite directions, creating a wider access point, ideal for larger openings. Both systems are popular for residential and commercial applications, enhancing aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
