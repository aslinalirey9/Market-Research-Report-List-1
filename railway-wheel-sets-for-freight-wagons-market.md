<p><p>Railway wheel sets for freight wagons are crucial components that provide stability and durability. They are primarily categorized into forged and casting wheels. Forged wheels are produced by shaping metal under high pressure, resulting in stronger, more resilient products that can withstand heavy loads and rough conditions. In contrast, casting wheels are formed by pouring molten metal into molds, which can be more cost-effective but may sacrifice some strength. Both types play vital roles in maintaining efficient freight transportation.</p></p>
