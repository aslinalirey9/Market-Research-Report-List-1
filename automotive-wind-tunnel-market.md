<p><strong>The Automotive Wind Tunnel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Circuit</li><li>Open Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wind tunnel market is segmented into two main types: closed circuit and open circuit wind tunnels. Closed circuit wind tunnels recirculate air through a closed loop, providing consistent airflow and enhanced control over testing conditions. They are ideal for precise aerodynamic studies. In contrast, open circuit wind tunnels draw air from the environment, allowing for larger scale testing but with less control over variables. Each type serves distinct purposes in automotive design and aerodynamics research, catering to different testing needs.</p></p>
