<p><p>The Embedded ADS-B market is divided into two main segments: launchers and receivers. Launchers refer to the platforms or aircraft that transmit Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast (ADS-B) data, providing real-time position information to improve air traffic management. Receivers, on the other hand, are devices that capture this broadcast data, enhancing situational awareness for air traffic controllers and other aircraft. Together, these components facilitate safer and more efficient air travel by enabling better tracking and communication within the aviation sector.</p></p>
