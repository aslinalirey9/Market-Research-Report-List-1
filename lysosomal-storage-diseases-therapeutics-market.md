<p><p>The Lysosomal Storage Diseases Therapeutics Market is categorized into several treatment types. Enzyme Replacement Therapy (ERT) replaces deficient enzymes, effectively alleviating symptoms. Stem Cell Therapy aims to restore normal enzyme production by introducing stem cells that differentiate into enzyme-producing cells. Substrate Reduction Therapy (SRT) reduces the accumulation of harmful substrates in the body, addressing the underlying issue of enzyme deficiency. Other approaches may include gene therapy and pharmacological chaperones, offering additional strategies for managing these complex genetic disorders.</p></p>
