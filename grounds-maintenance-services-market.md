<p><p>Grounds maintenance services encompass two primary market types: corrective maintenance and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance involves addressing issues as they arise, such as repairing damaged lawns, landscaping, or infrastructure. This reactive approach aims to restore grounds to their optimal condition. In contrast, preventive maintenance focuses on proactive measures to prevent potential problems, such as regular lawn care, pest control, and seasonal clean-ups. This approach enhances the longevity of landscapes and facilities while improving overall aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
