<p><p>The cylinder tie rod market comprises two main types: hydraulic and pneumatic cylinder tie rods. Hydraulic cylinder tie rods are designed to withstand high pressures and provide structural integrity in hydraulic systems, making them essential in heavy machinery and industrial applications. Conversely, pneumatic cylinder tie rods are used in systems that operate on compressed air, focusing on lighter-weight applications such as automation and robotics. Both types ensure reliable performance and durability, catering to their specific operational environments.</p></p>
