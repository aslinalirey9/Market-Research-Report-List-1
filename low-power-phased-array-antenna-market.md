<p><p>The Low Power Phased Array Antenna market comprises two primary types: Millimeter Wave and Microwave Phased Array Antennas. Millimeter Wave Phased Array Antennas operate in the frequency range of 30 GHz to 300 GHz, ideal for high-speed communication and precise radar systems. In contrast, Microwave Phased Array Antennas typically function within 1 GHz to 30 GHz, commonly used in telecommunications, satellite communication, and defense applications. Both types enable beam steering without physical movement, enhancing transmission efficiency and versatility.</p></p>
