<p><strong>The Diesel Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Diesel Injection System</li><li>Indirect Diesel Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diesel injection system market primarily consists of two types: direct diesel injection and indirect diesel injection systems. </p><p>Direct diesel injection involves injecting fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency and power output while reducing emissions. This system is typically used in modern engines for better performance. </p><p>In contrast, indirect diesel injection injects fuel into a pre-combustion chamber, allowing for better mixing and smoother combustion. This method is often found in older engines, offering reliability but generally lower efficiency compared to direct systems.</p></p>
