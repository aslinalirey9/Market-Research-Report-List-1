<p><p>The portable air purifier market comprises several types, each utilizing different technologies to improve air quality. HEPA filters efficiently capture microscopic particles, while activated carbon removes odors and volatile organic compounds. Static electricity purifiers charge particles, causing them to clump and settle. Ionizers emit charged ions to attract pollutants, and ozone generators produce ozone to eliminate odors and bacteria. Other types may include UV light purifiers and combination systems, offering diverse options for consumers seeking cleaner indoor air.</p></p>
