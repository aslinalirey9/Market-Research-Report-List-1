<p><strong>The Internal Pipe Coating Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotating Pipe</li><li>Non-Rotating Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The internal pipe coating equipment market is categorized into two main types: rotating and non-rotating pipe systems. Rotating pipe equipment utilizes a mechanism that moves coating materials evenly along the pipe's interior surface, ensuring uniform application. Conversely, non-rotating pipe systems rely on stationary setups where the coating is applied through fixed nozzles or spraying mechanisms. Both types serve various industries, such as oil and gas, water treatment, and construction, enhancing the pipes' corrosion resistance and improving overall durability.</p></p>
