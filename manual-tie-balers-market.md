<p><p>The manual-tie balers market is primarily divided into vertical and horizontal types. Vertical balers operate in a compact space, efficiently compressing materials like cardboard and plastic for easy handling and transportation. They require manual tying of bales after compression. Horizontal balers, on the other hand, handle larger volumes and feature a conveyor system, allowing continuous feeding and automatic tying, making them ideal for high-capacity operations. Each type caters to specific operational needs and space requirements in waste management and recycling sectors.</p></p>
