<p><p>The Low Smoke Halogen Free Flame Retardant Polypropylene market is categorized based on specific testing standards: IEC60332-1, IEC60332-2, and IEC60332-3. IEC60332-1 evaluates vertical flame propagation in insulated wires. IEC60332-2 focuses on the potential for flame spread of single vertical cables. IEC60332-3 assesses the behavior of bundles of cables in a vertical arrangement. These standards ensure safety in applications where reduced smoke emission and flame retardancy are critical, particularly in construction, automotive, and electronics industries.</p></p>
