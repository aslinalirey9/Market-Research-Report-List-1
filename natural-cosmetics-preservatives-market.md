<p><p>The natural cosmetics preservatives market is categorized into four main types: lipids, acids, alcohols, and others. Lipids, such as plant oils and butters, provide moisture and longevity to formulations. Acids, like citric and salicylic acids, inhibit microbial growth and extend shelf life. Alcohols, including ethanol and phenethyl alcohol, act as antimicrobial agents, enhancing product stability. The "others" category encompasses botanical extracts and antioxidants, which also serve preservative functions while providing additional skin benefits, appealing to the growing demand for clean beauty products.</p></p>
