<p><strong>The Malfunction Indicator Light Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intermittent Malfunction Indicator Light</li><li>Continuous Malfunction Indicator Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Malfunction Indicator Light (MIL) market can be categorized into two main types: intermittent and continuous. An intermittent malfunction indicator light signals temporary engine issues, activating sporadically based on specific conditions, which may confuse users regarding the severity of the problem. In contrast, a continuous malfunction indicator light remains illuminated, indicating persistent or critical engine faults requiring immediate attention. Understanding these differences helps consumers and industry professionals assess vehicle health and prioritize maintenance, ultimately enhancing vehicle performance and safety.</p></p>
