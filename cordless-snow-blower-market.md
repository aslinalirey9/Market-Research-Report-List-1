<p><p>The cordless snow blower market features three main types based on performance and design. Single-stage cordless snow blowers are lightweight and ideal for quick snow removal on smaller driveways and sidewalks. Two-stage cordless snow blowers offer enhanced power and efficiency, capable of handling deeper snow and larger areas, using an auger and an impeller for optimal discharge. Three-stage cordless snow blowers provide the highest performance, employing an additional auger for faster clearing and handling heavy, wet snow with ease, suitable for extensive snow removal tasks.</p></p>
